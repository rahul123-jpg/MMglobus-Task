About Up & Personal Podcast

In an effort to stay relevant and connected with the real world, Maximilian Globus is launching the Up & Personal podcast. This series features inspirational and revolutionary thoughts from real people, offering listeners insights that help them succeed beyond their current boundaries. By drawing connections in the real world, Up & Personal aims to inspire and challenge listeners to think differently and achieve more.
